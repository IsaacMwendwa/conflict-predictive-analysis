Background Information

Big Data Analytics have been applied in various use-cases, with an intention of providing insights which can drive decision-making, policy reforms and strategic planning.

The objective of this study is to give a proof of concept that big data analytics can be applied to conflict data and predict the future occurrences of conflict events in Kenya.
