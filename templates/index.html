Predictive Analysis of Conflict Events Incidence in Kenya

Background Information

Big Data Analytics have been applied in various use-cases, with an intention of providing insights which can drive decision-making, policy reforms and strategic planning.

The objective of this study is to give a proof of concept that big data analytics can be applied to conflict data and predict the future occurrences of conflict events in Kenya.

Predictive Analysis of Conflict Events Incidence in Kenya

Welcome to do a Predictive Analysis of Conflict Events Metrics in Kenya. The project comprises of two models which are in their default (not optimized) configuration. One model functions to predict the most probable Event Types to happen in the prediction period. The other model seeks to predict the Counties projected to have a high level of Conflict Events Incidence in the prediction period.

The prediction problem is formulated as: Given the future date for which predictions are to be performed, predict the Event Types and the Most Probable High Incidence Counties, in the prediction period.

Specifying Prediction Date

Please proceed to enter a Prediction Date in the field labelled "Prediction Date".

The system will then use predictive models to predict Event Types and the Most Probable High Incidence Counties, in the prediction period
